    About Us

    We are a FTC team based in Portland, Oregon. Our goal is to push this sport to its limits, both in technical and outreach elements. To this effect, we are prepared to bring a wild and challenging approach to the 2012-2013 FTC season.

    About FTC

    FTC is a program under FIRST -- For the Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology -- which is devoted to teaching high school students about science and technology fields and career. I is a coopertition, where teams work both with and against each other to create small robots to solve a yearly challenge.
